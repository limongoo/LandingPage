About Us

reFRESH was built to bring the magic of CSS to everyone; not just the back-end developer. We wanted to make the process of building landing pages and choosing fonts/colors as simple and fluid as possible. As easy as it looks, making things look clean can be a maddening, mind-wrenching process. The LIT squad (Langston,Ivan & Tanya) took it upon themselves to tackle this task for the betterment of the greater coding society. That being said, thank YOU for visiting our site. Sit back, relax, and don't forget... reFRESH
